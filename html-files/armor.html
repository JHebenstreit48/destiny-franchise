There are 5 different rarity levels of armor and weapons in Destiny 2. The following are the rarity levels in order from the most common to the most rare.

  • Common (White background)
  • Uncommon (Green background)
  • Rare (Blue background)
  • Legendary (Purple background)
  • Exotic (Gold/Yellow background)
