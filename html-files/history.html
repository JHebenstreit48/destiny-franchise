The very first Destiny video game was released on June 12, 2014. The sequel known as Destiny 2 or D2 for short. Was first released on September 6, 2017. Both games have had several DLC's (Downloadable Content) released to expand upon the lore or in game story every year since it was first released. There have also been lore books or volumes that have been physically released to consolidate into what is known as the "Grimoire Anthology".


In the game you play a character known as a "Guardian". The guardians are basically a near immortal being with paracausal powers bestowed on them by a floating AI beings known as Ghosts. The ghosts were created by a planet or moon sized entity known by many names. Humanity refers to this entity as "The Traveler". However, there are alien species from other planets and solar systems that have other names for it. It is not well known what The Traveler actually is. However, via the ghosts your guardian gains various paracausal powers and can be continuously revived by its ghost. The first 10 years of this game focused around the Light vs. Darkness saga. This means the story focuses around your guardian and the other characters it encounters in a decade long story about the war between the forces of The Light (sky) and the forces of Darkness (deep).


Over the course of 10 years. You eventually find out the forces of darkness are controlled by an entity known as "The Witness". You also later find out The Witness is not a singular entity. It is an entity composed of an entire alien race that was the first species to be touched by The Traveler. However, The Traveler eventually left the home world of The Witness. In anger and in a form of retaliation for The Traveler abandoning them. The entire species chose to form themselves into one entity that would become known as The Witness.
