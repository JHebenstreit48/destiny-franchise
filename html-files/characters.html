Your Guardian

Your guardian is a paracausal entity, that was once dead but revived by a floating AI known as a Ghost.

Your Ghost

Your ghost is a floating AI companion, created by a giant planet or moon shaped entity. It bestows upon you various paracausal powers or abilities. It is even able to resurrect you in most situations. This means your character is near immortal.

Commander Zavala

Commander Zavala is known as the leader of The Last City vanguard. He is also the vanguard leader for the in game titan class. He is from a human species known as the Awoken.
